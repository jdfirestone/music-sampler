Griz's Brings Fresh Frunk to Electronic Music

by Josh Firestone

Photo of Griz Performing Live by Adam Rogan on Flickr.

Griz, born Grant Kwiecinski, is an electronic-funk musician from Michigan. Pairing his raw talent on the saxophone with a remarkable command of electronic producing, Griz is known for his distinctive soulful future-funk sounds. There aren’t many artist like him in the very crowded world of electronic music and that’s why I’m so drawn to his unique style. Griz’s live performances are notable for his energetic saxophone solos and incredibly loyal fan base. If you haven’t heard any of his work yet, I beg you to give any of his songs a listen. I promise you his work will not disappoint.
